Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers to Your Queries

How do I make a reservation?
To make a reservation, simply visit our website and use the booking form on the homepage. Select your pickup and drop-off locations, dates, and desired vehicle type. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and debit cards. Some locations may also accept cash or digital payment methods. Please check with your specific rental location for details.
Can I modify or cancel my reservation?
Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation through our website or by contacting our customer service team. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on how close to the pickup time you make changes.
What documents do I need to rent a car?
You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in the renter's name, and proof of insurance. International renters may need to provide a passport and an international driver's permit.
Is there an age requirement for renting a car?
The minimum age to rent a car is typically 21 years old. However, drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Some specialty or luxury vehicles may have higher age requirements.
